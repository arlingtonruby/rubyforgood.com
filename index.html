About Ruby for Good

Ruby for Good is an annual event based out of the DC-metro area where Ruby programmers from all over the globe get together over a weekend to build projects to help our communities. The conference will take place at George Mason University with the participants staying in the dorms and brainstorming, hacking, and cooking together in communal areas. We'll be working on projects that help our communities. We do this in our free time just for the fun of giving back! If you have any questions please drop us a note.

Down to Business

In 2015 we are super excited to be helping the Humane Society of Fairfax, the Denver GrowHaus, the World Wildlife Fund, the Purple Door, All Income Foods, Baltimore Arts, the US Vote Foundation, and the RSpec-Rails team. See what past participants have said about the projects we've built. If you have a project suggestion or would like to lead a team, we would love to hear from you!

What you need to know

You just need to bring yourself, your laptop, and whatever you plan on wearing. You will get your own dorm room complete with linens, pillow, blanket and towels so you don't have to worry about hauling that stuff with you. We'll also be supplying food, drinks, and fun atmosphere. If you have a particular board game or card game that you would like to bring please do so... we can only guarantee that we'll have the equipment needed to play Artemis.

What does it cost?

Tickets are $150 all-inclusive; a big thank you to our sponsors for offsetting the cost of registration! Registration includes room, board, linens, pillows, towels, swag, and all food (contact us with any dietary restrictions). Preparing food is considered a team building exercise so all meals, as well as the clean up after the meals, will be done by teams.

Anything else?

Even though the conference runs from July 31st through August 2nd, attendees are welcome to arrive on July 30th and stay through August 3rd. Make a vacation out of it! (Or buy the cheaper plane tickets.) We need to know if you're coming early/staying late by the last day of ticket sales - July 14, 2015. There will be food available for those coming early and staying late, just provide a heads up so there is enough for all.
